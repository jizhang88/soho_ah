About Us

As a manufacturer we are able to meet and fulfill the different needs of customers regarding on how they want their towel exactly. We are able to achieve this with our dedicated team in Georgia, Arizona, China, and Pakistan. Our AH owned factories and plants in China and Pakistan have been manufacturing for the textile industry around the globe for over 30 years.

Our headquarters located in Georgia along with our warehousing facility in which we keep replenished inventory all year so we can always meet demand of our customers. We have a sourcing and logistical office in Arizona, that provide further assistance to our private label customers in the mid-west and West Coast. With experience, dedication and a hardworking team, we can offer customers private labeling, variety of towels and other textile products at good quality and price points.
